Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical program last?

Programs vary from single 2-hour workshops to multi-session interventions spanning several weeks. We customize duration based on your institution's needs and goals.

Are your programs age-appropriate?

Yes, all our content is tailored to specific grade levels and developmental stages. We conduct programs from Grade 6 through Grade 12.

What is the group size for workshops?

We typically work with groups of 30-50 students per session for optimal interaction. Larger groups can be accommodated with multiple sessions.

Do you provide parent programs?

Yes, we conduct parent awareness sessions when requested by schools. These can be standalone or complement student programs.

What are your credentials?

Conscious Nurturing is founded by 5 certified psychiatrists. All our trainers are certified professionals with expertise in adolescent mental health.

How do we measure program effectiveness?

We use pre and post-program assessments, feedback forms, and follow-up consultations to measure impact and outcomes.
